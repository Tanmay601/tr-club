About Our Club .... .- --

The HAM CLUB OF RVCE is dedicated to the exciting field of amateur radio. We are a community of students passionate about wireless communication, electronics, and global connectivity. Our club provides a platform for members to learn about radio theory, build antennas, operate transceivers, and participate in various on-air activities.

Our mission is to foster a deep understanding of radio technology, promote responsible amateur radio operation, and encourage experimentation. We organize regular workshops for licensing exams, practical sessions on circuit building, and participate in national and international amateur radio events like Field Day.
